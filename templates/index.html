About HAR

This project envisions a system that has a dual purpose use:
Drowsy Driver Detection & Fight Detection.

  • Drowsy Driver Detection is used to detect drowsiness and alert the driver to take a break. It analyzes the driver's behavior, such as facial expressions, head and eye movements, and steering patterns to detect signs of drowsiness, such as drooping eyelids and alerts the driver to take a break.

  • Fight Detection aims to provide an automated system for detecting aggressive and violent behavior.This application of human activity recognition has the potential to significantly enhance campus safety and security measures.

    • The scope of our current project is limited to alerting drivers when they start to feel drowsy preventing road accidents and detecting small fights occurring in campus.
    The suggested applications can be used to save lives and property
